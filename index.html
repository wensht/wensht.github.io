Short Bio:

    Dr. Shiting Wen is currently a Professor in the School of Computer and Data Engneering, NingboTech University. He received his Ph.D degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), and City University of Hong Kong (CityU), respectively, in 2012. Prior to joining NingboTech University, he worked at City University of Hong Kong as research assistant.
    Dr. Wen's research interests include Internet of Things, Big Data Processing and Analysis, Cloud Computing, Database. He is currently a reviewer of World Wide Web Journal (WWWJ), Journal of Web Engineering (JWE), ACM Transactions on Internet Technology (TOIT), Service Oriented Computing and Applications (SOCA), and so on. He now is served as a Program Committee (PC) Member of International Conference on Human Computing (HumanCom 2012), and International Conference on Web Engineering (JWE 2013).
