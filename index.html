Short Biography

Shiting Wen is a Professor and Vice Dean in School of Computer and Data Engineering at NingboTech University. He received his Ph.D degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China (USTC), and City University of Hong Kong (CityU), respectively, in 2012. Prior to joining NingboTech University, he worked at City University of Hong Kong as research assistant. His research interests include Internet of Things, Big Data Processing and Data Mining, Cloud Computing, Database.
He has served in the Technical Program Committee of various international conferences including KDD, WSDM, DASFAA, ECMLPKDD, WISE, WAIM, ADMA, ICWL and so on.
